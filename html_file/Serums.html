FAQs

Common questions about our products

+

What do face serums do?

Formulated with a concentrate of active ingredients, they help target different skin problems, while moisturizing and protecting it! Our 3 new face serums respectively work on soothing redness and dryness, brightening dull and uneven skin tone and refining the skin and targeting enlarged pores.

+

Which face serum should I choose?

Our 3 face serums are suitable for all skin types, even the most sensitive. So, depending on your needs, you can choose the serum that suits you.

♡ The Soothing Face Serum is formulated with Tasmanian pepperberry, Arnica & Hyaluronic Acid to help soothe redness and dry skin.

♡ The Brightening Face Serum is formulated with Dragon fruit extract, Kakadu plum (rich in vitamin C) and Squalane to help brighten dull skin and uneven skin tone.

♡ The Refining Face Serum is formulated with Niacinamide, thyme, Camelina and Hyaluronic Acid to help target enlarged pores and skin texture.

+

How often should I use a face serum?

You can use our face serums daily, morning and/or night! You can also incorporate them by occasion into your beauty routine.

+

How do I apply a face serum?

Day and night, apply a small amount of serum to clean skin and gently massage into the skin using circular and patting motions. Follow with a face cream to maximize the effectiveness of the serum.

Beauty tip: Put the serum in the refrigerator before application for a fresh effect!

*A drop or two of serum should suffice. The dropper should never touch your face or hands directly.

+

Why isn't the serum bottle full?

The bottle can hold up to 60 ml of serum, but is only filled with 48 ml to prevent the glass stem from breaking if the product freezes during delivery. In bulk, the bottle can be filled up to 60 ml. :)
