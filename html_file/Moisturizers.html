FAQs

Common questions about our products

+

What are the benefits of face moisturizers?

Face moisturizers help to replenish and maintain the skin's hydration levels by trapping moisture in the outermost layer of the skin. This prevents dryness and keeps the skin feeling soft, smooth, and supple. Additionally, moisturizers help strengthen the skin's natural barrier function, keeping it looking youthful and protecting it from external aggressors. Regular use of a moisturizer can also improve overall texture and tone, promoting a healthier, more radiant complexion.

+

Which face moisturizer should I choose?

You should pick your face moisturizer based on your skin type and needs!

The Floral Face Moisturizer with Hyaluronic Acid is suitable for any skin type, especially dry, sensitive or mature skin. It contains flower waters, hibiscus oil and hyaluronic acid to brighten the complexion, improve skin elasticity and nourish the skin deeply. Texture: Soft and light

The Marine Algae Face Moisturizer is ideal for oily, combination or blemish prone skins. It contains aloe vera, algae, Chlorella and Melissa flower water to to calm and revitalise the skin and boost suppleness. Texture: Gel-like

The Superfruit Face Moisturizer with Bakuchiol is ideal for very dry and/or mature skin. It contains mango butter, pomegranate seed oil and Bakuchiol to provide a wonderful hydration, prevent blemishes and keep skin looking youthful! Texture: Rich and decadent

+

How often should I use a face moisturizer?

You can use a face moisturizer daily, morning and night for best results!

+

How do I apply a face moisturizer?

After cleansing the face, take a small amount of your moisturizer and apply it to the face and neck in circular motions.

Please note that you should always apply lighter skin care before your moisturizer.
