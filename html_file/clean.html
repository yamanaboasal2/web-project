FAQs

Common questions about our products

+

What is a Makeup Remover Bar?

A Makeup Remover Bar is a solid skincare product designed to effectively remove makeup while being gentle on the skin. It's a convenient and eco-friendly alternative to traditional liquid makeup removers.

+

How do I use a Makeup Remover Bar?

Simply wet the bar and the face with lukewarm water. Rub the bar between your hands to make it lightly lather and apply the makeup remover cream to the face. Massage in circular movements and rinse.

You can follow up with a Konjac Sponge to deeply cleanse the skin after removing makeup.

+

What is a Konjac Sponge?

Konjac Sponges are plant-based and biodegradable sponges made from Konjac root fibers. Its irregular texture allows to cleanse the skin without soap or cleanser.

+

How do I use a Konjac Sponge?

Wet the konjac sponge with warm water until it becomes soft. Massage the skin by making circular movements. Splash your face with water to rinse. Also, rinse the sponge and hang it with the string to let it dry between uses.

After 2 or 3 months of use (or 100 uses), put the konjac sponge in the compost.

+

Why should I use a Makeup Remover Bar and a Konjac Sponge?

Cleansing the skin with a Konjac Sponge after removing makeup is an important step in skincare!

While makeup removers are effective at breaking down and lifting makeup from the skin's surface, they may leave behind traces of makeup residue, along with dirt, oil, and environmental pollutants. Cleansing the skin afterward ensures that all remaining impurities are thoroughly removed, leaving the skin clean, refreshed and ready for any skin care products.
